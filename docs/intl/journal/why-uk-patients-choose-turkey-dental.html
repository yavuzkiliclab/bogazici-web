Why do UK patients choose Turkey for dental work?

Published 17 July 2026· 6 min read

Hundreds of thousands of UK patients have travelled to Turkey for dental treatment. That is not an accident or a fad. There are clear, practical reasons behind the trend, and understanding them helps you judge whether the same reasons apply to you. This article explains honestly why UK patients choose Turkey, and where those reasons have limits.

The cost, above all

The biggest driver is straightforward: cost. Private dental treatment in the UK is expensive, and for larger work like implants or a set of veneers the figures are high enough to put treatment out of reach for many. Turkey offers the same treatments at a fraction of the price, even after travel. For people who want work the NHS will not provide but cannot justify the private-UK cost, Turkey opens a door that was otherwise closed. This is the single most common reason, and an honest one.

The honest driver

Cost opens the door. Quality, speed and now aftercare are what make patients walk through it.

Access to treatment the NHS will not provide

NHS dentistry has become hard to access in many areas, and it does not routinely cover implants or cosmetic work. For a great deal of the treatment people want, the NHS is simply not an option, which leaves expensive private care or Turkey. For many, that framing is what prompts them to look abroad in the first place. It is less about choosing Turkey over the NHS and more about the NHS not offering the treatment at all.

Shorter waiting times

Waiting is the other push factor. Complex private work at home can mean delays, and NHS waits for what it does cover can be long. Turkey can often schedule treatment within weeks, with the main work done in a planned trip. For someone living with a dental problem that affects daily life, being treated sooner has a real, immediate value that pure cost comparisons miss.

Specialised, high-volume clinics

Turkey's scale means many clinics are highly specialised in exactly the treatments international patients seek, with in-house laboratories and efficient, well-drilled processes. Patients are drawn to clinics that do their specific treatment often and well. This concentration of experience is a genuine pull, not just a cost story.

Aftercare that now reaches home

Historically, the one thing that gave patients pause was aftercare: what happens back home. This has been the strongest argument against travelling, and an honest one. It is also the reason a real UK partner network matters so much. In-person aftercare at eight UK locations removes the biggest remaining hesitation, which is why more patients now feel able to choose Turkey with confidence. Where the reasons have limits

These reasons are real, but they are not universal. They apply most to larger treatments and least to small routine work. And every one of them depends on choosing a good clinic: the cost saving, the quality, the aftercare all evaporate at a poor one. So UK patients choose Turkey for sound reasons, but the smart ones choose a licensed, quality clinic within Turkey, which is the decision that really determines the outcome.
